Admissions

At North West Technological University, we are committed to making the admissions process as seamless and supportive as possible. We welcome applications from diverse backgrounds and strive to create an inclusive community where every student can thrive.

Undergraduate Admissions

Our undergraduate programs are designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in their chosen fields. Prospective students can apply through the Central Applications Office (CAO) or directly via our university portal. We consider academic performance, personal statements, and extracurricular involvement during the selection process.

Apply via CAO

Postgraduate Admissions

We offer a wide range of postgraduate programs, including taught courses and research degrees. Applicants are required to submit academic transcripts, letters of recommendation, and a research proposal (for research degrees). International students are encouraged to apply and will find dedicated support services to assist with their transition.
