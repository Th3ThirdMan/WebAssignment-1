About Us

North West Technological University was founded in 1987 with a mission to drive innovation and empower future leaders. Our programs combine academic excellence with real-world experience, fostering creativity, problem-solving, and collaboration. With over 10,000 students across multiple disciplines, NWTU continues to lead in sustainability, technology, and research.

Our Vision

The University aspires to be a global leader in technological innovation, sustainability, and inclusive education. We envision a future where our graduates drive positive change, advance knowledge, and contribute to a more equitable and sustainable world through creativity, research, and collaboration.

Our Mission

At North West Technological University, our mission is to cultivate a learning environment that inspires innovation, critical thinking, and global citizenship. We are committed to providing students with cutting-edge education, hands-on research opportunities, and industry collaboration, empowering them to become leaders and problem-solvers who positively impact their communities and the world.

Our Core Values

Innovation

Driving creativity and discovery through cutting-edge research and education.

Community

Fostering inclusion and diversity within and beyond our campus.

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in every aspect of learning and leadership.
